About Us

Kaalaama Charitable Trust is a non-governmental organization based in Gundaipet that is dedicated to improving the lives of the underprivileged. The foundation’s primary focus is on healthcare, education, and livelihood programs that aim to alleviate poverty and create a more sustainable future for communities in need.

With a team of dedicated professionals and volunteers, Kaalaama Charitable Trust is committed to empowering individuals and communities through education and training, providing access to quality healthcare, and creating livelihood opportunities that can help families break the cycle of poverty. Through its various initiatives, the foundation has impacted the lives of many individuals and families in Gundaipet and continues to work towards a better tomorrow for all.

Chairperson & Founder

Durgam Shyam Rao

Mr. Durgam Shyam Rao is the Chairperson and Founder of Kaalaama Charitable Trust. A multifaceted individual, he has dedicated his life to serving the community and making a difference in the lives of the underprivileged.

His commitment to social service is evident through his leadership in the trust, where he focuses on healthcare, education, and livelihood programs aimed at alleviating poverty and fostering a sustainable future for communities in need. As the founder, Mr. Shyam Rao laid the groundwork for many impactful initiatives that continue to serve marginalized groups.

With an unwavering vision for a better society, Mr. Durgam Shyam Rao leads with compassion and determination. His work extends beyond the professional realm, and his dedication has positively impacted the lives of many individuals and families in the region, particularly in Gundaipet.
